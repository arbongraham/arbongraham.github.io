ENGLISH PREMIER LEAGUE ANALYTICS

Hypothesis:

The teams that have won the Premier League since 2016 will rank at the highest of all these questions. (Liverpool, Manchester City 2x, Chelsea)

Questions to be answered:

What teams in the premier league have the highest average SPI in their games across the data set?

What team in the premier league have have the highest probability of winning across the data set?

What team in the premier league had the highest predicted goal total across the set?

What was the actual total goals for that team?

Why did I want to know this?

The reason I wanted to analyze this is I have a personal interest in soccer and wanted to see how teams might compare across these statistics over a four year period. I specifically chose the Premier League as that is the league I'm most comfortable with.

What did I use to analyze the data?

I used 'Numbers' in on Mac to sift through and sort the data I wanted to gather to get the information.

How did I do this?

The data was obtained from Five Thirty Eight which has gathered data over the past four years and uses ESPN / Five Thirty EIght’s Soccer Power Index as a rating for the teams originally devised by Nate Silver. A link with more detail on how their data was gathered and projected made can be found here.

The first step was to download a data set, unfortunately this contained over 44,000 values that included scores from leagues all over the world. My first step was to narrow this down to only the premier league and I did this just by filtering by “league_id”.
